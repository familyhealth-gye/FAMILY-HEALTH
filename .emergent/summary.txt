<product_requirements>
The Family Health application is a medical-administrative system for a clinic in Guayaquil, Ecuador, designed to centralize management across specialties like odontology, general medicine, nutrition, psychology, and gynecology. Initially, it provided patient registration (name, ID, age, phone, specialty, doctor, date/time, payment, observations) and appointment scheduling (date, time, doctor, service), including a WhatsApp button for manual patient messaging.

The application has been expanded to include:
- **Facturación (Billing):** Form for recording invoices (not electronic generation), including invoice number, patient, doctor, specialty, service, value, date, payment type. It also features a searchable list of invoices by patient, doctor, month, or number, with monthly totals and a CSV/Excel export option.
- **Inventario Médico (Medical Inventory):** An editable table for medical supplies with fields for name, category, quantity, unit cost, and minimum stock. It supports manual entry/exit movements and visual alerts for low stock. Automatic deduction of materials used in treatments is planned, but currently manual.
- **Pagos a Doctores (Doctor Payments):** Calculates doctor payments based on an editable percentage of services rendered. It provides a monthly report showing doctor, total services, total billed, percentage, total payable, and payment status (Pending/Paid).
- **WhatsApp Automático (Automatic WhatsApp):** Generates direct WhatsApp links with pre-filled confirmation messages for appointments (e.g., Hola [nombre del paciente], tu cita en Family Health ha sido agendada para el [fecha] a las [hora]).
- **Acceso y Diseño:** Implements JWT-based login with user roles (Admin, Doctor), professional medical design (azul #00a8cc, verde menta #6decb9, blanco #f5faff), includes a Family Health logo, and is fully responsive.
- **Doctores (Doctors):** Registration now includes specialty and subspecialty, with configurable percentages for payments.
- **Pacientes y Citas (Patients and Appointments):** Patients are registered with comprehensive details. The system supports a flow where patients schedule appointments, which a doctor can then initiate to begin attention.
- **Módulo de Usuarios (User Module):** An admin-only interface to create and manage users, assigning roles (e.g., Doctor) that control access to different modules. Doctors created here can log in using their credentials.
- **Historias Clínicas (Clinical Histories):** Includes a general clinical history. Specific forms for Medicina General and Pediatría have been prioritized and implemented, allowing doctors to record patient data according to their specialty. The latest update integrates prescription generation directly within these clinical history forms, changing plan de tratamiento to receta and observaciones.
- **Recetas Médicas (Medical Prescriptions):** Generates prescriptions (with CIE-10, diagnosis, medications, doses, precautions) and exports them to PDF with the clinic logo. This is now directly linked to the clinical history forms.

Authentication is required, with roles for access control. The objective is to consolidate all functionalities into a single application for internal control, with future integrations planned for SRI invoicing and advanced WhatsApp automation.
</product_requirements>
